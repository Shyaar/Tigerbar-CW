About Tiger Beer

Tiger Bar, your go-to destination for exceptional brews and great company. Established in 1988, we proudly serve Tiger beer, known for its bold flavor and refreshing finish. Our inviting atmosphere is perfect for unwinding after a long day or celebrating with friends, with regular events like beer tastings and live music that foster a vibrant community of fellow beer lovers. Cheers to unforgettable moments at Tiger Bar!
